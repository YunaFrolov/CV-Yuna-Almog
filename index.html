CanarIT - College Final Project

A System for Monitoring Gas Leaks and Fires

Cooking gas is widely used in Israel. Gas heating is a cheap and efficient way to cook and to heat water. However, the use of cooking gas carries many dangers that can lead to injury and heavy damage to property. This system comes to report gas and fire leaks in real time and alert the relevant parties quickly. As a result, it will be possible to prevent damage to life and property and to provide information to emergency forces and to prevent the presence of people trapped in the arena.
